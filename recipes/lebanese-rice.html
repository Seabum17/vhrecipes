Lebanese Rice

A traditional lebanese preparation of rice served at most dinners.

Authentic middle eastern rice is made of only three ingredients;

rice, vermicelli noodles, and olive oil.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice

    1/2 cup vermicelli durum wheat semolina

    2 tablespoons extra virgin olive olive

    1/2 teaspoon salt

    dash cinnamon optional

    parsley optional for garnish

Instructions

  1. Rinse the rice with cold water until the water runs clear. Drain well and set aside.

  2. In a medium non-stick pot, heat the olive oil on medium heat. Add the vermicelli

    pasta and cook, stirring frequestly until all the vermicelli is a deep golden brown

    color. Be careful not to burn it.

  3. Transfer thericeoverthecookedvermicelli, and stir to combine and coat the rice

    with the olive oil. Season with saltand a dash of cinnamon, if desired.

  4. Add 4 cups water and bring the mixture to a boil. The water will reducein the

    process. Reduce theheat tolow, cover thepot and coook for 15 minutes.

  5. When the rice is fully cooked, remove fromtheheat and allow the rice to steam for

    5 minutes. Then uncover and fluff with a fork.

  6. Serve warm with fresh parsley and toasted pine nuts.
